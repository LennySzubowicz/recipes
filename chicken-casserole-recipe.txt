Ingredients:
 - 3 or 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
 - 1 can of condensed chicken soup
 - 1/2 cup of milk
 - 8 oz shredded cheddar cheese
 - 2 cups of Pepperidge Farm herbed stuffing

Directions:
 1. Use a 9"x12" oven-safe glass dish
 2. Mix condensed cream of chicken soup with milk
 3. Coat the bottom of the glass dish with a thin film of the
    cream of chicken soup
 4. Cut up the chicken breasts into bite-sized chunks
 5. Spread the cut up chicken in the glass dish
 6. Pour the rest of the condensed chicken soup mix over
    the chicken
 7. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese evenly over the
    chicken.
 8. Sprinkle the herbed stuffing evenly over the chicken
 9. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes
